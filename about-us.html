About Us

the creators of unExplore

UnExplore is the brain child of Colin Hayhurst and Rhys Poulton. Developed by them in the summer of 2016 it is still in its early stages.

Colin Hayhurst works at the University of Sussex in the Innovation Centre, with a history of enabling development in physics and engineering he brings this knowledge to sussex to aid and further the research done by faculty and students.

Rhys Poulton was a university student studying Physics. He developed his programming skills beyong the scope of his course which he then used to create UnExplore from its infancy using Javascript, HTML and CSS. Even today, after completing his studies at Sussex he still paticipates and helps lead the progress of unExplore alongside Colin.
