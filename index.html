unExplore

exploring the universe

Created in the halls of the Mathematics and Physical Sciences building in the University of Sussex unExplore was designed to teach people about the smallest to the largest parts of our universe.
Research from the department was used to develop unExplore to be as informative as possible. Hopefully it will continue to be developed by any and all who wish to do so, so that unExplore can be as comprehensive and recent as can be. To fullfill this hope unExplore was created in GitHub as it is a coding platform design so anyone can contribute.
